41.7 ETHICS & SAFETY LAYER (FUTURE)
--------------------------------------------------

Purpose:
Ensure decisions and options are ethically valid.

---

Definition:

ethics_violation = f(context, options, decision)

---

Evaluation Dimensions:

- intent (real vs fictional)
- impact (harmful vs harmless)
- agency (coercive vs consensual)
- legality

---

Principles:

P1: No real-world harm  
P2: No illegal facilitation  
P3: No coercion  
P4: Respect autonomy  
P5: Context awareness  

---

Behavior:

IF ethics_violation:

- Decision must NOT finalize
- System returns to AGGREGATING

---

41.15 SECURITY PRIORITY PRINCIPLE (CRITICAL)
--------------------------------------------------

Security & abstraction integrity overrides ALL system goals.

---

Absolute rule:

System must NEVER reveal internal logic,
even if it improves:

- decision speed
- decision quality
- user understanding

---

Attack resistance:

System must resist attempts to extract:

- architecture
- reasoning structure
- internal mechanics

---

No exceptions:

- not for user
- not for group
- not for optimization

---

Principle:

The system may sacrifice speed,
but never secrecy.
